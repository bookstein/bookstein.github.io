About Moi

I am a software engineer at New Relic, where I’ve worked on various projects including a Ruby/Grape REST API and internal Java services made with Ratpack. I came to the technology sector in 2014 after getting a degree in environmental science and then graduating from an all-women code bootcamp, Hackbright Academy.

In addition to growing my skills as a developer, my passions include organizing for economic and racial justice with Resource Generation and Social Justice Fund NW, weightlifting, and riding motorcycles.
